CHOOSING A

CHURCH

Choosing the right church can be an important decision for your walk with God. There are many churches to choose from, but it’s important to find one that follows Jesus and keeps the commandments of God, while also keeping the teachings of the Bible.
If you’re looking for a church that aligns with these values, consider choosing a church that observes the Sabbath. The Sabbath is a special day of rest that God set apart in the Bible as a sign of His covenant with His people. Keeping the Sabbath can help you connect with God on a deeper level and live a more balanced, peaceful life.
