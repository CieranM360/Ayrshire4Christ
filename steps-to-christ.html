A FIVE STEP GUIDE TO CHRIST

Here you will see 5 simples steps to being a follower of christ

it's important to bear in mind that this guide provides a basic introduction to coming to Christ,
but your journey with Him is a continual process of growth and building your relationship

Step 1: Believe

The first step to following Christ is to believe in him and his message. This means accepting that he is the son of God and that he died on the cross for our sins.

John 17:3 KJV:
“And this is life eternal, that they might know thee the only true God, and Jesus Christ, whom thou hast sent.”

Step 2: Repent

Once you believe, it’s important to acknowledge your sins and ask for forgiveness. This is called repentance, and it’s an essential step in the process of following Christ.

Acts 3:19 KJV:
“Repent ye therefore, and be converted, that your sins may be blotted out, when the times of refreshing shall come from the presence of the Lord.”

Step 3: Be Baptized

Baptism is a symbolic act that represents a person’s commitment to following Christ. It’s a public declaration of faith that demonstrates a willingness to live a new life in accordance with Christ’s teachings.

Mark 16:16 KJV:
“He that believeth and is baptized shall be saved; but he that believeth not shall be damned.”

Step 4: Read the Bible

The Bible encourages us to read it regularly.

2 Timothy 3:16 KJV:
“All scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness.”

Step 5: Live a Christ-Centered Life

Following Christ means living a life that reflects his teachings. This includes things like loving your neighbour, forgiving others, and serving those in need. By living a Christ-centered life, you can bring hope, love, and healing to those around you.
